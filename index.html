Q & A

  • Where can I find out what we are doing this week, homework, assignment, etc?
    • A weekly agenda will be posted in Canvas on our classes start page. If anything changes during the week the agenda will change to reflect that.
  • Where are the most accurate grades?
    • All grades will be posted to PowerSchool. PowerSchool will have the most accurate grades and may be different than a canvas grade. PowerSchool will be updated regularly and all missing/absent work be be given a zero until completed.
  • Where can I get extra help?
    • You may email me questions that you have that I can address the next day in class. I can usually meet students before school from 8:05ish to 8:50am on most days and after school by appointment. We also will have a Flex period on Wednesdays starting in September where you can come in for help.
  • Where can I go if I misplace any of my notes or I am at home and it would be helpful if I can see you work through an example again?
    • Check Canvas for all class notes and resources.
